Basic contact details, public-facing profile fields, and the stuff customers actually need.

Required

Payments

C. Legal Text

D. Domain, DNS & Platform Control

The ownership handoff section people always underestimate right up until the registrar account is tied to someone's ancient email.

Transfer

Transfer Reminders

  • Move billing ownership before changing deploy access.
  • Confirm who owns DNS before touching SSL or email records.
  • Export a backup of repo secrets and legal text before edits.
  • Make one person clearly responsible for registrar, hosting, and Git admin roles.

ABOUT THE OPERATOR

Meet Ralph Williams

The operator behind Gotta Know It Customs, focused on bold custom work, clean presentation, and helping customers build something that feels personal instead of off-the-shelf.

Ralph Williams

Operator • Gotta Know It Customs

Ralph Williams is building Gotta Know It Customs around the kind of custom work people actually remember. His approach leans into strong visual identity, customer connection, and builds that are meant to stand out on sight and hold attention when they pull up.

Based in Minneapolis, he is shaping the brand with a practical operator mindset and an eye for presentation. The focus is not just on getting work done, but on helping customers create a ride that feels like theirs, from wraps and lighting to audio-driven upgrades and full custom direction.

At the center of it is a straightforward idea: make the process easier to start, make the result worth talking about, and keep the business personal enough that customers feel like they are dealing with a real operator instead of a generic shop account.

What Drives the Brand

  • Bold, customer-facing custom work that looks intentional and marketable
  • Builds that balance style, sound, and overall visual presence
  • A hands-on operator voice instead of generic shop language
  • Clear communication from first contact through project follow-up
